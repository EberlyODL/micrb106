Upon completing this lesson you should be able to:

  • Describe the shapes and arrangments that bacteria can have
  • List and characterize the external structures of bacteria
  • Describe the significance of biofilms
  • Describe the composition of the bacterial cell wall
  • Explain the differences between gram positive and gram negative cells
  • Explain the steps of the gram stain and how it works
  • List and characterize the internal structures of bacteria
  • Describe the function of endospores and explain their importance
  • List the characteristics of the archea and eukaryotes
  • Describe the relative size of microbes and how magnification works
  • Explain resolving power and factors that influence it
  • Explain how staining works
  • Describe the types of specialized staining
  • Differentiate between the various types of microscopy
