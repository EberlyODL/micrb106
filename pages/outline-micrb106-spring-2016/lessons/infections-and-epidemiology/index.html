Upon completing this lesson, you should be able to:

  • Describe the relationship of organisms to human host
  • Explain the difference between pathogen and opportunistic pathogen 
  • List the steps of an infection
  • Describe the events of each stage of an infection
  • Explain the differences between endotoxins and exotoxins
  • Describe the concept of a reservoir
  • List the purposes of epidemiology
  • Define the basic terminology used by epidemiologists
  • Explain the aspects of descriptive epidemiology
  • Explain the meaning of epidemiological rates
