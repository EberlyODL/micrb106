Upon completing this lesson, you should:

  • Describe the parts that make up atoms.
  • List the common elements in the body with letter abbreviations.
  • Describe the behavior of ions.
  • Compare and contrast the 3 kinds of chemical bonds.
  • Describe the properties of water.
  • Define saturation.
  • Compare and contrast the role of acids and bases.
  • Define and list the major organic compounds.
  • Describe the structure and function of the 4 basic organic compounds.
  • Describe the role of enzymes.
  • Describe how ATP functions to provide energy in a cell.

This lesson focuses on a number of areas related to basic Chemistry. You should review each page in order as they build upon one another. Many of these topics will be review,while others may be new to you. Either way, you will learn the fundamentals of chemistry needed in this course.
