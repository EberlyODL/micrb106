After completing the modules on Cells, you should be able to:

  • Explain the differences between prokaryotic and eucaryotic cells
  • Explain the structure and function of the plasma membrane
  • Describe the concept of diffusion
  • Define concentration and it's relationship to diffusion
  • Describe the differences among the three types of membrane transport systems
  • Describe the processes of exocytosis and endocytosis
  • Explain osmosis and three cases of osmotic effects on cells
  • List the types and functions of main cellular organelles
  • Describe the structure and role of a chromosome
  • Describe a gene
