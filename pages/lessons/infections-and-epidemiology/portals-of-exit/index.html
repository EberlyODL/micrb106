Of course the final outcome of all this infection is, quite literally, life or death for the host. By this time either the immune response, perhaps with the aid of antibiotics leads to a recovery of the patient. In some cases, recovery from the illness can leave behing permanent organ damage as in meningitis. Just because there is an abatement of symptoms does not mean the pathogen have been ultimately destroyed either. It is possible for the disease go enter a dormant state called latency. This is particularly seen in viral infections like HIV or varicella (chickenpox), which can remain dormant for years and lead to a recurrence much later on.
