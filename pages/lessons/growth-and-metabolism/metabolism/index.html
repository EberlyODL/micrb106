Upon completing this lesson, you should be able to:

  • Explain the differences between Anabolism and Catabolism
  • Describe the major pathways used by cells to form ATP
  • Detail the role oxygen plays in choosing different energy producing pathways
  • Compare and contrast respiration versus fermentation

  • Explain the process of glycolysis
  • Describe the events of the Krebs cycle
  • Describe the events of the Electron Transport Chain
  • Describe how macromolecule synthesis pathways are interconnected
