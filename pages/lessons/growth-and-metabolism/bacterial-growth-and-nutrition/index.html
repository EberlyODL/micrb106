Upon completing this lesson, you should be able to:

  • Describe how bacteria acquire energy and sources of carbon
  • List the different groups bacteria fall under based on temperature
  • List the growth characteristics of bacteria based on oxygen availability
  • Describe other factors that influence bacterial growth
  • Explain how bacteria reproduce
  • Detail the pattern that bacterial reproduction follows
  • Explain the effects of exponential growth on generation time
  • Describe what happens at each stage of the bacterial growth curve
  • List the features of ways to count bacteria
